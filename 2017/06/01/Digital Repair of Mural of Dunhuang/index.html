Digital Repair of Mural of Dunhuang - 敦煌壁画数字修复VR体验

Jun 1 2017

This project is part of The National Basic Research Program (973 Program). The cooperation agency includes Dunhuang Academy China and other research groups. This project had 3 members. The final finished project is showed on the 2017 Graduate Exhibition of Masters in Academy of Arts and Design, Tsinghua University.

项目来源于国家重点基础研究发展计划(973计划)项目《雕塑、壁画类文化遗产的虚拟复原》。项目专注于将敦煌壁画修复过程利用 VR 技术向大众进行展示。从而让大众更加了解敦煌艺术及敦煌壁画数字修复过程。

主要负责 Unity 开发。
