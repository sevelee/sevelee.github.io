BOOM!

Jun 1 2014

BOOM! 射！ from 倪昕Ni Xin on Vimeo.

Modern people often feel nervous. This system is designed for people who have this kind of problems. The system will check the motion of people and show some pictures and paints on the projection.

There were 2 member for this project. Using somatosensory interaction, the installation guide people to jump to relax themselves. We used trampoline as the carrier, using Kinect to get the action of visitor, and the display on the floor by projector will change with the visitors’ action.

This project was showed at 2014 Graduate Exhibition of Academy of Arts and Design, Tsinghua University.

自主项目。项目成员 2 人。意在通过体感交互的方式,引导人们运动放松自己。以蹦床为 载体,通过 Kinect 捕捉人体动作。在人头顶的投影系统会根据人体动作做出相应反应。

项目最终在 2014 年清华大学美术学院本科生毕业作品展中展出。
