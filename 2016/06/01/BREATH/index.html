BREATH

Jun 1 2016

BREATH from LI Jiawei on Vimeo.

This is a digital art project.

The visitor use our mask to breath, in which there is a sensor detects the breath of visitors. Balloons containing pure air swings with the rhythm of visitor’s breath. But the more the visitor breathe, the fainter balloon swing.

作品利用气球营造出一个沉浸式体验的环境。参观者身处气球之间,感受 气球的上下运动随着自己的呼吸而运动。在气球群中体验自身呼吸对环境的影响。
